A · Bento progress board

Asymmetric tiles instead of rows. Hero bank gets a progress ring; secondary banks sit as smaller tiles. Feels like a study dashboard, not a settings list.

B · Level path / roadmap

Vertical journey: unlock from basics → hard passage → full exam. Turn “pick a bank” into a progression story. Strong for Thai learners who think in dungeons/levels.
