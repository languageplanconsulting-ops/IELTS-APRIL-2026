Grammar

ตอนนี้ 7.5
✓ Band 7 — simple tense + subordinating
ทำได้แล้ว: “even though she wasn’t born talented”
● Band 7.5 — perfect/past เริ่มปรากฏ
ทำได้แล้ว: “I have admired…”, “she worked really hard”
○ Band 8.0 — complex range error-free

“because she worked really hard, not because it was given to her”

○ Band 8.5+ — conditional + passive ไม่มี error
ใส่ passive: “she is widely regarded as one of the greatest witches.”
