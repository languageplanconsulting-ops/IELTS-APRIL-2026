# Cambridge Listening Sections 2-4 Bank

## Purpose

This file is a `section-first` content bank for Cambridge IELTS Listening Books `18-20`.

It is designed for:

- `Listening Vocabulary Builder`
- question upload planning
- section-by-section curriculum building
- turning Cambridge listening into vocabulary + paraphrase drills

Important note:

- This is a `derived study bank`, not a full verbatim reproduction of every Cambridge page.
- Each entry keeps:
  - the `test`
  - the `section`
  - the `question coverage`
  - the `answer key`
  - the `core vocabulary / paraphrase focus`

Source pattern used throughout:

- practice pages from IELTS Training Online
- example format:
  - `https://ieltstrainingonline.com/practice-cam-18-listening-test-01-with-answer-and-audioscripts/`
